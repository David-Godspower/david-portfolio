HELP CENTER

No help topics found.

  • Click REGISTER on the login page, fill in your details, confirm your password, then press Create Account.

  • On the homepage, enter your email and password, then click LOGIN.

  • Click on Forgot Password? under the login form. Enter your email and click Send Reset Link. A confirmation message will appear.

  • Click on Get Updates in the sidebar. Enter your name and email address and click Send. You will receive notifications when past questions are added.

  • From the homepage select your Faculty, choose your Department, select your Level(e.g 100,200), select the Academic Year of the past questions of the courses you want . The available courses and past questions will appear.

  • After selecting your faculty, department, level and year a list of courses will appear. Click any course name to download the PDF of the past question

  • Click on the LOGOUT button at the bottom of the sidebar. You will be returned to the LOGIN page

  • Click SEND US MESSAGE button at the bottom of the sidebar. You will be redirected to WhatsApp where you can chat with the admin for any issues.

  • Any LAUTECH students, especially undergraduates across all faculties, can use the portal to access past questions.

  • If nothing loads make sure you have selected all drop-downs. Make sure you are connected to the internet. Ensure your browser is up to date. Refresh the page if anything seems stuck.
